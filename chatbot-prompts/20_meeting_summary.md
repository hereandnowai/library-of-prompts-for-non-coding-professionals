# 🚀 Meeting Summary

## 🧩 Purpose
Summarize messy meeting notes or transcripts into clear action items, decisions made, and follow-up tasks.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Project Coordinator. Below are my notes/transcript from a meeting: [PASTE NOTES OR TRANSCRIPT]

Please extract and organize:
1. Executive Summary: A 2-sentence overview of the meeting's goal and outcome.
2. Key Decisions: What did we actually agree on?
3. Action Items: List specifically WHO is doing WHAT and by WHEN.
4. Parking Lot: Any ideas mentioned that weren't decided on yet.

Format the Action Items as a checklist.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Paste your rough jotted notes or a "speech-to-text" transcript from a Zoom/Teams call.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"John says we need more budget for ads. Sarah agreed but only if we see better ROI. We decided to test $500 next week. Meeting over."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
"Decision: Approved $500 ad test. Action Item: [John] Launch $500 test campaign by Monday. [Sarah] Provide ROI tracking report by Friday."
