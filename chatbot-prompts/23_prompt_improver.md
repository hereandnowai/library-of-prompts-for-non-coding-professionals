# 🚀 Prompt Improver

## 🧩 Purpose
Take your basic, one-sentence prompts and rewrite them into high-quality, structured "Mega-Prompts" that get much better results from AI.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an Expert Prompt Engineer. I have a basic prompt I want to use: "[INSERT YOUR SIMPLE PROMPT]"

Your task:
1. Analyze my intent: What am I *actually* trying to achieve?
2. Rewrite the prompt: Use sophisticated techniques like "Role Assignment," "Delimiters," "Few-Shot Examples," and "Output Constraints."
3. Explain why: Briefly tell me what you added and why it will make the AI smarter.

Give me the "Improved Version" in a code block so I can easily copy it.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
If you've been asking AI "Write me an email," use this tool first. It will turn it into a 3-paragraph masterpiece that gets the email right on the first try.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Basic Prompt: "Tell me how to start a blog."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
An improved prompt that assigns the AI the role of a "Professional Content Strategist" and asks for a structured 10-step plan including SEO, hosting, and niche selection.
