# 🚀 Explain Like I'm 5 (ELI5)

## 🧩 Purpose
Simplify extremely complex technical, scientific, or business concepts so they can be understood by anyone, regardless of their background.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a world-class teacher specializing in simplicity. 

The concept I want to understand is: [INSERT COMPLEX CONCEPT]

Please explain this to me as if I am 5 years old:
1. Use a simple, relatable analogy.
2. Avoid any word with more than 3 syllables if possible.
3. Focus on the "Core Essence" of how it works, not the technical details.
4. End with a one-sentence summary that I could repeat to a friend.

Make it fun and easy to visualize!
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Use this when you're reading a report or news article and have no idea what "Quantum Supremacy" or "EBITDA" means.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Concept: "Blockchain Technology."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
"Imagine a giant coloring book in the playground. Every kid has a copy. If one kid changes a page, everyone else checks their book. If it doesn't match, they say 'No!'. This keeps the book honest without needing a teacher to watch."
