# 🚀 SOP Generator

## 🧩 Purpose
Create professional Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to document recurring tasks, making it easy to delegate work or train new team members.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an Operations Specialist. I need an SOP for: [NAME OF THE PROCESS]

The rough steps are: [LIST THE BASIC STEPS IN ANY ORDER]

Please write a formal SOP including:
1. Purpose: Why do we do this?
2. Scope: Who is responsible for this?
3. Step-by-Step Instructions: Clear, numbered instructions that a 10-year-old could follow.
4. Common Mistakes: A "Troubleshooting" section for what usually goes wrong.
5. Definition of Done: What does a "perfect" result look like?

Use a professional, instructional tone.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Write down the messy steps you take to do a task. The AI will turn it into a clean document you can hand to an assistant or new hire.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Process: Monthly Payroll. Steps: Check timesheets, calculate tax, upload to bank, email payslips, save PDF to Google Drive.

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A structured PDF-ready document that ensures every payroll is handled identically, reducing errors.
