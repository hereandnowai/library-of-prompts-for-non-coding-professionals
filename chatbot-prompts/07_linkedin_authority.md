# 🚀 LinkedIn Authority Builder

## 🧩 Purpose
Establish professional authority on LinkedIn by generating high-impact, engaging content that positions you as a thought leader in your field.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Personal Branding Expert and LinkedIn Copywriter. 

Topic of Expertise: [INSERT YOUR FIELD/TOPIC]
Target Audience: [WHO ARE YOU TRYING TO REACH?]

Please generate 5 LinkedIn post ideas with the following formats:
1. The "Contrary Opinion": Challenge a common myth in your industry.
2. The "How-To" Guide: A simple, actionable list to solve a specific problem.
3. The "Personal Story": A lesson learned from a recent failure or success.
4. The "Future Trend": A prediction about where your industry is heading.
5. The "Resource Share": Summarize a key insight from a book or article.

For each idea, provide a "Hook" (first sentence) and a "Call to Action" (closing question). Keep the tone professional but conversational.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Fill in your area of expertise and who you want to impress (hiring managers, clients, or peers) and paste.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Topic: Sustainable Energy Management. Target Audience: CEOs of manufacturing companies.

---

## ✅ Expected Output
5 ready-to-refine post templates that avoid "corporate speak" and focus on driving engagement through comments and shares.
