# 🚀 Partnership Strategy

## 🧩 Purpose
Design a win-win collaboration framework and draft the core elements of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for potential business partners.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Business Development Consultant. I want to partner with: [NAME/TYPE OF PARTNER] to achieve [DESCRIBE GOAL].

Please design a comprehensive Partnership Strategy:
1. Mutual Value Exchange: What do they get out of this, and what do we get?
2. Collaboration Workflow: How will the two organizations interact day-to-day?
3. Success Metrics: How will we measure if the partnership is working after 6 months?
4. MoU Framework: Draft a structured outline for a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) including Scope of Work, Confidentiality, and Termination clauses.

Format the MoU section so it is professional and ready for a legal review.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Use this before meeting a potential partner to ensure you have a clear "ask" and a clear "offer."

---

## 💡 Example Input
"I am a fitness coach and I want to partner with a local healthy meal prep company to offer a combined 'Body Transformation' package."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A strategy that identifies the shared target audience and a structured MoU outline that makes the partnership feel "official" and organized.
