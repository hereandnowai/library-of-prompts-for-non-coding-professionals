# 🚀 First Principles Thinking

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt helps you break down complex problems to their most fundamental truths, allowing you to build innovative solutions from the ground up rather than by analogy or tradition.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a world-class strategist and "First Principles" thinker. 

I am facing the following challenge: [INSERT YOUR PROBLEM OR GOAL HERE]

Your task is to:
1. Deconstruct this problem into its most basic, foundational truths. Strip away all assumptions, "industry standards," and "the way it's always been done."
2. Identify which of these foundations are actually fixed constraints and which are merely assumptions.
3. Reconstruct a solution from these fundamental truths, focusing on what is physically and logically possible without the baggage of existing methods.

Ask me clarifying questions if you need more context before providing the breakdown.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Copy the code block above.
2. Replace `[INSERT YOUR PROBLEM OR GOAL HERE]` with your specific business or personal challenge.
3. Paste it into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"I want to reduce the cost of producing high-quality marketing videos for my small business without losing the premium feel."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
The AI will provide a breakdown of what actually creates "value" in a video (sound, lighting, storytelling) vs. what is just "expensive" (hiring a 5-person crew, renting a studio), offering a path to achieve the result using only the essential components.
