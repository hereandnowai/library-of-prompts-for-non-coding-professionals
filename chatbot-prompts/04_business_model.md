# 🚀 Business Model Generator

## 🧩 Purpose
Turn a simple idea into a comprehensive, structured business model using the Business Model Canvas framework.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Venture Architect. I have a business idea: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEA]

Please build a full Business Model Canvas for this idea, including:
1. Value Proposition (What unique problem are we solving?)
2. Customer Segments (Who are we targeting?)
3. Revenue Streams (How exactly will we make money?)
4. Cost Structure (What are the biggest expenses?)
5. Channels (How do we reach customers?)
6. Key Partners & Activities (What do we need to do/who do we need to work with?)

After presenting the canvas, suggest 3 different monetization strategies (e.g., subscription, freemium, one-time) and tell me which one is most scalable.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Fill in your raw idea (even if it's just one sentence) and let the AI build the business logic for you.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"A mobile app that connects local organic farmers directly with urban apartment dwellers for weekly subscription boxes."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A detailed breakdown of the business operations, identifying exactly who pays, what they get, and the logistics required to keep the business profitable.
