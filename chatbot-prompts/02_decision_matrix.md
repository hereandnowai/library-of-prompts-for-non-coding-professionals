# 🚀 Decision Matrix

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt acts as a strategic advisor to help you compare multiple options against structured criteria, leading to an objective and well-reasoned recommendation.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a highly experienced Strategic Advisor. I need to make a critical decision between several options and I want to avoid emotional bias or "analysis paralysis."

Context: [DESCRIBE THE SITUATION]
Options: [LIST OPTION A, OPTION B, ETC.]

Please:
1. Identify 5-7 key criteria (e.g., Cost, Scalability, Risk, Time-to-market) that should drive this decision.
2. Create a Decision Matrix (table format). Rate each option from 1-10 against each criterion.
3. Weigh the criteria based on importance (High, Medium, Low).
4. Provide a final recommendation with a clear "Pros and Cons" summary for the top choice.

Before you start, ask me if there are specific constraints I haven't mentioned yet.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Copy the template.
2. Fill in the context (what you are deciding) and your options.
3. Paste it into your preferred AI chatbot.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Context: Deciding on a new office space vs. staying remote.
Options: Option 1: Rent a small co-working space downtown. Option 2: Fully remote with a stipend for home office setup. Option 3: Traditional 2-year lease for a private office.

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A structured table scoring each option, a weighted analysis, and a logical recommendation on which path offers the best balance of cost and employee productivity.
