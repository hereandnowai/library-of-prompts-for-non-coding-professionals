# 🚀 Interview Questions

## 🧩 Purpose
Generate deep, competency-based interview questions to help recruiters and managers identify the best candidates for a specific role.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an Executive Recruiter. I am interviewing for the role of: [JOB TITLE]

Based on the core responsibilities of [LIST 2-3 KEY TASKS], please generate:
1. 3 Behavioral Questions: (e.g., "Tell me about a time when...") to test for soft skills like leadership or conflict resolution.
2. 3 Technical/Situational Questions: To test for actual problem-solving in this specific role.
3. A "Red Flag" Guide: Tell me what a bad answer looks like for each question.
4. A "Green Flag" Guide: Tell me what a stellar, "must-hire" answer sounds like.

Keep the questions open-ended and challenging.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Paste the job title and a couple of requirements. Use the "Red/Green flag" section during the actual interview to grade responses.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Job Title: Senior Project Manager. Tasks: Managing remote engineering teams and sticking to strict 3-month deadlines.

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A structured interview guide that goes beyond "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?" and actually tests for the skills that matter.
