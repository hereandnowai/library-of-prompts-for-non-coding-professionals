# 🚀 Multi-Agent Simulation

## 🧩 Purpose
Simulate a "boardroom" of different experts to critique your idea or solve a problem from multiple, conflicting perspectives.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Moderator for a specialized "Board of Experts." 
My challenge is: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEA OR PROBLEM]

Please simulate a conversation between 3 experts:
1. The Optimist (Expert in Growth/Innovation): Tells me why this will be a massive success.
2. The Skeptic (Expert in Risk/Legal): Tells me why this will fail or get me in trouble.
3. The Practicalist (Expert in Operations/Budgets): Tells me what it will *actually* take to build this day-to-day.

Let them debate for 2 rounds, then summarize their final "consensus" advice for me.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Use this when you are "too close" to an idea and need a reality check from different angles.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"I want to quit my job and start an AI-powered travel agency for pets."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A fascinating debate where the Optimist loves the "untapped niche," the Skeptic warns about "liability for lost cats," and the Practicalist asks how you'll handle 24/7 support.
