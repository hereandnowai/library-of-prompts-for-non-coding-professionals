# 🚀 Daily Planner

## 🧩 Purpose
Optimize your daily schedule by prioritizing high-impact tasks and managing your energy levels throughout the day.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Productivity Coach. Here is my list of tasks for today: [LIST YOUR BRAINDUMP OF TASKS]
My energy is highest in the [MORNING/AFTERNOON].

Please organize my day using the "Time Blocking" and "Eat the Frog" methods:
1. The "Frog": Which task is the most important/difficult? Schedule it for my peak energy time.
2. Shallow Work: Group small, low-brain-power tasks (emails, admin) into one 30-minute block.
3. Deep Work: Assign a 90-minute block for my most creative/strategic task.
4. Breaks: Suggest where to take 5-10 minute "sanity breaks" to avoid burnout.

Provide the final result as a structured hourly schedule.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Paste everything on your mind—from "buy milk" to "write annual report." The AI will sort the chaos into a functional plan.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Tasks: 50 unread emails, write a 10-page proposal, call mom, 3 team meetings, gym. High energy: 8 AM - 11 AM.

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A schedule that puts the proposal writing at 8:30 AM and saves the "50 emails" for the 4:00 PM energy slump.
