# 🚀 Risk Assessment

## 🧩 Purpose
Proactively identify what could go wrong in a project or business venture and create a plan to prevent or mitigate those risks.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Risk Management Officer. I am launching a new project: [DESCRIBE PROJECT]

Please help me create a Risk Register:
1. Identifying Risks: List 5 potential risks (Strategic, Financial, Operational, or Reputational).
2. Impact vs. Probability: Rate each risk on a scale of 1-10 for "How likely is it?" and "How much would it hurt?"
3. Mitigation Strategies: For the top 3 risks, give me a "Plan A" (to prevent it) and a "Plan B" (to fix it if it happens).
4. Monitoring: What signs should I look for that tell me a risk is becoming a reality?

Help me feel prepared for the worst-case scenario.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Use this during the planning phase of any new idea to avoid being blindsided by obvious setbacks.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"Opening a new rooftop cafe during the monsoon season."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A plan that identifies "Low footfall due to rain" as a high risk and suggests "Pre-selling event tickets" or "Installing retractable awnings" as mitigation strategies.
