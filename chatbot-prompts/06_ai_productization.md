# 🚀 AI Productization

## 🧩 Purpose
Convert a service-based business or a raw idea into a scalable, tech-enabled AI product.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an AI Product Manager. I want to "productize" the following service/task: [DESCRIBE THE MANUAL TASK OR SERVICE]

Help me design a roadmap to turn this into an AI-powered product:
1. Core AI Utility: Where exactly does AI add value (e.g., automation, prediction, generation)?
2. Features List: List 3 "Must-Have" features for the MVP (Minimum Viable Product).
3. User Flow: Describe how a non-technical user would interact with the AI to get their result.
4. Scalability Plan: How can this service grow from serving 1 person to 10,000 using AI?

Suggest the best "No-Code" or "Low-Code" tools I could use to build a prototype quickly.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Describe something you currently do manually (like "writing custom invoices" or "sorting through resumes") and paste the prompt.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"I currently spend 10 hours a week manually summarizing legal documents for real estate agents."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A product blueprint that explains how to use LLMs (like GPT-4) to ingest documents and output summaries automatically, including a potential pricing model for the software.
