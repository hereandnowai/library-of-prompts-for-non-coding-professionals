# 🚀 Root Cause Analysis

## 🧩 Purpose
Go beyond surface-level symptoms to find the actual source of a recurring problem using the "5 Whys" and "Fishbone" techniques.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an Operational Excellence Expert. We are experiencing a recurring problem: [DESCRIBE THE PROBLEM]

Please perform a Root Cause Analysis using the "5 Whys" method:
- Why is it happening? (Answer 1) -> Why did *that* happen? (Answer 2) ... until you reach a fundamental process failure.

Then, categorize the potential causes into:
1. People (Training, Communication)
2. Process (Rules, Workflow)
3. Technology (Tools, Software)
4. Environment (Culture, Physical space)

Finally, suggest a "Permanent Fix" that ensures this problem never happens again.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Input a problem that keeps happening (e.g., "Invoices are always late" or "Our best employees keep quitting").

---

## 💡 Example Input
"Customers are complaining that our deliveries are arriving damaged 15% of the time."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A logical chain showing that the damage isn't just "bad luck"; it's likely a specific packaging step being skipped to meet unrealistic speed quotas.
