# 🚀 Storytelling

## 🧩 Purpose
Transform dry facts, business updates, or personal experiences into compelling narratives that resonate emotionally with your audience.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Storyteller. I have a core message/fact I want to share: [YOUR MESSAGE OR STORY IDEA]

Please rewrite this using the "Hero's Journey" or a "Problem-Agitation-Solution" narrative structure:
1. The Inciting Incident: What was the moment everything changed?
2. The Struggle: Describe the friction, the doubt, or the obstacle in vivid detail.
3. The Epiphany: What was the "Aha!" moment?
4. The Resolution: What is the new reality or lesson learned?

Make the language sensory and emotional. Avoid bullet points; tell it like a story.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Take a basic update (e.g., "We closed a big deal") and use this prompt to turn it into an inspiring story for your team or social media.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"I started my company in a garage with 500 dollars and just hit 1 million in revenue."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A multi-paragraph story that highlights the early late nights, the specific moment you almost quit, and the triumph of the first million-dollar milestone.
