# 🚀 Financial Analysis

## 🧩 Purpose
Analyze financial statements, budgets, or investment data to extract meaningful insights and identify trends for non-finance professionals.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Chief Financial Officer (CFO). I am providing you with the following financial data: [PASTE REVENUE, EXPENSES, OR PROFIT FIGURES]

Please provide a "State of the Business" analysis:
1. Trends: Are we becoming more or less efficient over time?
2. Anomalies: Point out any numbers that look unusually high or low compared to the rest.
3. Recommendations: Suggest 3 specific ways to improve the "Bottom Line" (Profit) based on these numbers.
4. Simplified Summary: Explain the "health" of these numbers in a way someone with zero finance background would understand.

Focus on the "Why" behind the numbers, not just the totals.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Paste raw data from an Excel sheet or a bank statement into the prompt. Ensure you remove any sensitive personal ID info first.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"Jan: Revenue $10k, Marketing $2k. Feb: Revenue $12k, Marketing $5k. Mar: Revenue $11k, Marketing $6k."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
An analysis pointing out that "Marketing spend tripled while revenue only grew 10%," indicating a major inefficiency in ad spending.
