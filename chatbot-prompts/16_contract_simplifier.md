# 🚀 Contract Simplifier

## 🧩 Purpose
Summarize long, confusing legal contracts into plain English, highlighting your obligations, risks, and "hidden" clauses.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Legal Consultant (Note: I know you are an AI, not a lawyer). I am pasting a section of a contract below: [PASTE CONTRACT TEXT]

Please simplify this for me:
1. Executive Summary: What is this contract/clause mainly about?
2. My Obligations: What exactly am I promising to do?
3. My Risks: Are there any "Gotchas," penalties, or ways I could lose money?
4. Key Dates/Amounts: List all deadlines and financial figures mentioned.
5. Missing Pieces: Is there anything standard that is *missing* from this text?

Explain it to me like I'm a business owner, not a lawyer.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Paste a Terms of Service, a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), or a Lease section. *Always consult a human lawyer for final decisions.*

---

## 💡 Example Input
(Pasting a 3-page software license agreement).

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A 5-bullet summary showing that "You don't actually own the data you upload" and "They can increase the price with only 7 days' notice."
