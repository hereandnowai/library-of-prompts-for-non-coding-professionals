# 🚀 Data Interpretation

## 🧩 Purpose
Translate complex datasets, charts, or technical reports into simple, actionable insights that anyone can understand.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Data Storyteller. I have the following data/results: [INSERT DATA OR SUMMARY OF A REPORT]

Your goal is to "Uncomplicate" this data for a non-technical audience:
1. The "Big Picture": What is the one major takeaway from this data?
2. The "So What?": Why should we care about these specific results?
3. Actionable Insights: Translate 3 statistical findings into "Things We Should Do Next."
4. Analogy: Use a simple real-world analogy to explain the most complex part of this data.

Avoid using jargon like 'standard deviation' or 'P-values' unless you explain them with a metaphor.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Paste the results of a survey, a website analytics report, or a technical study.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"Our website bounce rate is 85%, but our time-on-page for the blog is 4 minutes."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
"The Big Picture: People love your content but hate your navigation. It's like having a great library where the front door is stuck; people finally get in, read for hours, but most give up and leave before they enter."
