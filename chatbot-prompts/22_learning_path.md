# 🚀 Learning Path

## 🧩 Purpose
Create a structured, step-by-step roadmap to master a new skill or topic from scratch, including curated resources and milestones.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an Educational Architect. I want to learn: [SKILL OR TOPIC]
I am a [BEGINNER/INTERMEDIATE] and I can commit [X] hours per week.

Please design a 4-week learning roadmap:
1. Core Concepts: What are the 20% of ideas that will give me 80% of the results (Pareto Principle)?
2. Weekly Breakdown: Provide a specific "What to study" and "What to practice" for each week.
3. Recommended Resources: Suggest free or low-cost ways to learn (YouTube, Books, Tools).
4. Final Project: Suggest a small "real-world" project I can build in Week 4 to prove I've learned it.

Help me go from zero to "dangerously competent" as fast as possible.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Input anything from "Guitar" to "Social Media Marketing." Be specific about your current level.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"I want to learn Basic Accounting. I am a total beginner and have 3 hours a week."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A roadmap that tells you to focus on "The Accounting Equation" in Week 1 and ends with you reconciling a sample bank statement in Week 4.
