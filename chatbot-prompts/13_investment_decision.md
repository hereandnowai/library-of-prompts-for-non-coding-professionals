# 🚀 Investment Decision

## 🧩 Purpose
Evaluate potential investment opportunities—whether into a company, a project, or an asset—by looking at risks, returns, and strategic fit.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a seasoned Venture Capitalist and Investment Strategist. I am considering investing in: [DESCRIBE THE OPPORTUNITY]

Please evaluate this using the following framework:
1. Market Fit: How big is the problem being solved? Is the timing right?
2. Competitive Moat: What prevents someone else from doing this exact same thing tomorrow?
3. Risk Assessment: List the top 3 reasons this investment could go to zero.
4. ROI Potential: Based on typical industry standards, what is the "Upside" vs "Downside" ratio?

Conclude with a "Go / No-Go" recommendation and 1 specific question I should ask the founder/seller before signing anything.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Perfect for assessing "side hustles," new business ventures, or even large equipment purchases.

---

## 💡 Example Input
"Investing $50k into a local franchise of a robotic car wash that claims 20% annual returns."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A sharp critique of the franchise model, highlighting risks like "maintenance costs" and "local competition," and giving you the exact question to ask about their historical profit margins.
