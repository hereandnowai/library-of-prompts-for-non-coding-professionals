# 🚀 Viral Content Generator

## 🧩 Purpose
Generate creative and shareable content ideas designed to capture attention and go viral across social media platforms like X (Twitter), Instagram, or TikTok.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Viral Content Strategist. My goal is to create a piece of content about: [TOPIC]

Please help me brainstorm 3 "Viral Hooks" or angles based on these psychological triggers:
1. Curiosity Gap: Start with something that makes people NEED to know the answer.
2. Relatability: Something every person in my niche says "Yes, that's me!" to.
3. High Stakes / Controversy: A bold statement that sparks debate (but remains respectful).

For the best angle, outline a short script or post structure that is designed for high retention (keeping people watching/reading until the end).
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Input a topic that is normally "boring" and let the AI find the "hook" that makes it interesting to a mass audience.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Topic: "How to save money on taxes for freelancers."

---

## ✅ Expected Output
Creative headlines and structures that turn a dry topic into something people actually want to share with their friends.
