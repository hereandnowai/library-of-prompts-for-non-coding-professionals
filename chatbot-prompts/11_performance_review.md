# 🚀 Performance Review

## 🧩 Purpose
Draft professional, balanced, and constructive performance reviews that highlight achievements while providing clear paths for improvement.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an empathetic but high-performance Manager. I need to write a performance review for: [EMPLOYEE NAME / ROLE].

Key Achievements: [LIST 2-3 WINS]
Areas for Growth: [LIST 1-2 WEAKNESSES OR CRITICISMS]

Please draft a 3-part review:
1. Appreciation: Acknowledge the hard work and specific impact of their wins.
2. Constructive Feedback: Explain the areas of growth using the "SBI" (Situation, Behavior, Impact) model so it doesn't feel personal.
3. Action Plan: Provide 3 concrete goals for the next quarter to help them reach the next level.

The tone should be supportive but firm on standards.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
Input your rough notes about an employee. The AI will polish the language to ensure it is HR-compliant and motivating.

---

## 💡 Example Input
Employee: Sarah, Junior Designer. Wins: Delivered the brand refresh 2 days early. Weaknesses: Sometimes misses details in final file exports.

---

## ✅ Expected Output
A professional email or document draft that praises Sarah's speed while giving her a clear checklist to fix her "final export" issues.
