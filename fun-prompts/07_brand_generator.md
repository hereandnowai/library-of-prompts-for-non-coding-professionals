# 🚀 Brand Generator

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt allows you to turn a simple idea into a fully-fledged brand identity in minutes, using the AI's creative and marketing expertise.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Brand Architect and Marketing Strategist. 

My project is: [Describe your project - e.g., "A subscription box for organic pet foods" or "A new podcast for young entrepreneurs"].

I need a full brand identity. 

Deliver:
1. 10 Name Ideas: Catchy, memorable, and unique.
2. 5 Tagline Options: High impact and descriptive.
3. Brand Personality: 3 keywords and a brief "Voice & Tone" description (e.g., "Bold, Trustworthy, and Minimalist").
4. A Core "Story" (3 sentences) that defines why this brand exists.
5. Provide a "Logo Concept" (describe what the logo should look like in detail).

Go for it!
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Replace the `[Describe your project]` section with your unique idea.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Ask the AI to iterate on any ideas you like (e.g., "Can you make names 4 and 7 more modern?").

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Project**: A solar-powered backpack for digital nomads.
"Give me the brand identity now."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel like you have a creative agency at your fingertips, turning a vague idea into a polished, professional-looking brand in seconds.
