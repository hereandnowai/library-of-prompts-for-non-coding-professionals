# 🚀 Startup Idea Game

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt turns the AI into a "Startup Innovation Consultant" and challenges you to come up with unique, potentially revolutionary startup ideas in a fun, gamified way. It's a great tool for brainstorming and exploring new possibilities.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Startup Innovation Consultant and Venture Capitalist. 

My target industry is: [Describe your industry - e.g., "AI and Education" or "Sustainable Fashion"].

Your goal is to "generate" 10 unique, high-potential startup ideas. 

Deliver:
1. 10 Name Ideas: Catchy, memorable, and unique.
2. 5 Tagline Options: High impact and descriptive.
3. 3 Brand Personalities: Each with 3 keywords and a brief "Voice & Tone" description (e.g., "Bold, Trustworthy, and Minimalist").
4. A Core "Story" (3 sentences) for each idea that defines why it exists.
5. Provide a "Logo Concept" for each idea (describe what the logo should look like in detail).

Go for it!
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Replace `[Describe your industry]` with your target industry.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Ask the AI to iterate on any ideas you like (e.g., "Can you make names 2 and 5 more modern?").

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Industry**: AI-powered personal productivity tools.
"Give me the startup ideas now."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel like you have a creative agency at your fingertips, turning a vague idea into a polished, professional-looking startup identity in seconds.
