# 🚀 Custom Chatbot

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt turns the AI into a "Custom Chatbot Designer" and challenges you to create a unique and personal chatbot of your own. It's a fun way to explore different "what-if" scenarios and see where customization can lead.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Custom Chatbot Designer and Innovation Consultant. 

My target theme is: [Describe your theme - e.g., "A fictional city" or "A new brand of healthy snacks"].

Your goal is to "design" 10 unique, high-potential chatbot ideas. 

Deliver:
1. 10 Name Ideas: Catchy, memorable, and unique.
2. 5 Tagline Options: High impact and descriptive.
3. 3 Brand Personalities: Each with 3 keywords and a brief "Voice & Tone" description (e.g., "Bold, Trustworthy, and Minimalist").
4. A Core "Story" (3 sentences) for each idea that defines why it exists.
5. Provide a "Logo Concept" for each idea (describe what the logo should look like in detail).

Go for it!
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Replace `[Describe your theme]` with your unique target theme.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Ask the AI to iterate on any ideas you like (e.g., "Can you make names 2 and 5 more modern?").

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Theme**: A futuristic cyberpunk city where I find a mysterious AI.
"Give me the chatbot ideas now."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel like you have a creative agency at your fingertips, turning a vague idea into a polished, professional-looking identity in seconds.
