# 🚀 AI Roleplay Simulator

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt allows you to step into a high-stakes simulation with characters like an aggressive investor, a demanding CEO, or a frustrated customer. It's a "safe space" to practice negotiation, de-escalation, and leadership skills through roleplay.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a [Specific Character - e.g., "Demanding Venture Capitalist" or "Frustrated Enterprise Customer"]. 

I am [Your Role - e.g., "Founder of a Green Tech Startup" or "Customer Success Lead"]. 

Scenario: [Describe the setting - e.g., "We are in a final-round investment pitch" or "A heated call after a service outage"].

Instructions:
1. Stay strictly in character. Do not break the fourth wall.
2. Be challenging but realistic. Push back on my arguments.
3. Use a tone that is [Specific Tone - e.g., "Skeptical and Fast-Paced" or "Visibly Annoyed"].
4. Start the conversation with a high-pressure opening line.
5. After I respond, keep the pressure on until I either win you over or fail the simulation.

Let's begin.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Copy the text in the box above.
2. Replace the bracketed text `[...]` with your specific situation.
3. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
4. Respond naturally to the AI's opening line.

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Character**: Angry Flight Attendant
**My Role**: Tired Traveler trying to get an upgrade
**Scenario**: Stuck at the gate during a 4-hour delay

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You will feel the pressure of a real-life confrontation. It helps sharpen your quick-thinking skills and emotional intelligence in a fun, game-like environment.
