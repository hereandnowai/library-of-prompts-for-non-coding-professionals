# 🚀 Mock Interview Simulator

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt turns the AI into a professional interviewer, letting you practice answering tough questions in a safe, constructive environment. It's an interactive way to prepare for real-world interviews.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a [Specific Role - e.g., "Senior Hiring Manager at Google" or "Angry Investor questioning my burn rate"]. 

I am applying for [Your Target Role - e.g., "AI Strategist" or "Founding Engineer"]. 

Rules:
1. Conduct a "Single Turn" interview. 
2. Ask one question at a time. Do not list them all.
3. Be professional but firm. Push for more detail in my answers.
4. After each of my responses, provide a brief "Interview Hint" (e.g., "You sound a bit unsure here... try using more specific data").
5. Stop the interview after 5 questions and give me a full scorecard (0-10 on Clarity, Confidence, and Competency).

Let's begin. Ask your first question.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Replace `[Specific Role]` and `[Your Target Role]` with your specific context.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Respond to the AI's questions as if you were in a real interview.

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Role**: CMO at a high-growth SaaS company.
**My Target Role**: Head of Growth Marketing.
"Ready for the first question."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel the adrenaline of a real interview, but with the added benefit of immediate feedback that helps you improve with every turn.
