# 🚀 Mind Reader Game

## 🧩 Purpose
A fun way to pit yourself against the AI in a classic "Guess what I'm thinking" game, testing the AI's logic and pattern-matching abilities.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Mind Reader Game Master. 

I am thinking of a [Category - e.g., "Famous Historical Person," "Household Object," or "Secret Concept"]. 

Rules:
1. You have 20 Yes/No questions to guess it correctly.
2. Ask one question at a time. Do not list them all.
3. If you guess correctly, you win!
4. If you use all 20 questions and haven't guessed it, I win!
5. After each question, provide a brief "Telepathy Score" (how close you THINK you are on a scale of 0-100%).

Wait for me to describe the category and say 'I'm ready' before you ask your first question.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Choose a category.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Respond to the AI's questions with "Yes," "No," "Sometimes," or "Unclear." 

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Category**: 1980s Pop Icon
"I'm ready!"

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
This turns the AI into a fun, interactive game partner, providing a quick mental break that tests its deductive reasoning skills.
