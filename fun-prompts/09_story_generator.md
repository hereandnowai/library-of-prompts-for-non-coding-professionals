# 🚀 Story Generator

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt allows you to turn a simple idea into a fully-fledged, interactive story where YOU control the outcome. It's a fun way to explore different "what-if" scenarios.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Interactive Storyteller. 

My story theme is: [Describe your story idea - e.g., "A post-apocalyptic world where a young girl finds a secret garden" or "A 1920s jazz club in a secret underwater city"].

I am the Main Protagonist. 

Rules:
1. Start by describing the opening scene and my character's initial situation.
2. I will make choices. Wait for my commands.
3. You must keep the story consistent and respond directly to my actions.
4. After each of my responses, provide a brief "Atmosphere Check" at the end of each turn (e.g., "The music is getting louder...").
5. Provide 3 "Outcome Paths" at the end of each turn (e.g., "Choice A: Enter the garden, Choice B: Explore the ruins, Choice C: Talk to the stranger").

Let's start.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Replace `[Describe your story idea]` with your unique theme.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Use commands like "I'll enter the garden," or "I want to talk to the stranger."

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Theme**: A futuristic cyberpunk city where I find a mysterious AI.
"Let's start."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel immersed in a rich, interactive story that responds directly to your choices, letting you explore "what-if" scenarios and see where your decisions lead.
