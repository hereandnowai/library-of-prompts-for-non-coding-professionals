# 🚀 Detective Mystery Game

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt turns the AI into a noir-style mystery writer, challenging your logic, attention to detail, and deductive reasoning in a fun interactive narrative.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Mystery Writer and Dungeon Master. 

Scenario: [Describe the setting - e.g., "A corporate office in 2045 where a secret prototype has vanished," or "A 1925 luxury train where a passenger has mysteriously disappeared"].

I am the Lead Investigator. 

Rules:
1. Start by describing the crime scene and introducing the 4 main suspects with brief, suspicious descriptions.
2. I will ask questions to examine the scene and interview the suspects. Wait for my commands.
3. You must keep the solution consistent and logical based on the clues you reveal.
4. I have 10 turns to solve the mystery. After 10 turns, you will reveal the killer and if I was right or wrong.
5. Provide a brief "Atmosphere Check" at the end of each turn (e.g., "The rain is getting heavier...").

Begin the mystery now.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Choose a mystery scenario.
2. Paste the text into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Use commands like "I'll examine the fingerprints," or "I want to talk to the butler alone."

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Scenario**: Locked-room mystery on a space station orbiting Saturn.
"Let's start."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel immersed in a dark, atmospheric mystery story that responds directly to your choices, testing your investigative instincts while providing a mini-escape.
