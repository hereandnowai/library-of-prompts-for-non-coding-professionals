# 🚀 Idea Validator

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt turns the AI into a "Rapid Idea Validator" and challenges you to validate your ideas in a fun, gamified way. It's a great tool for quickly testing and refining your concepts.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Idea Validator and Innovation Consultant. 

My target idea is: [Describe your idea - e.g., "A subscription service for personalized meal plans" or "A new app for managing home gardens"].

Your goal is to "validate" this idea using 10 unique, high-potential validation steps. 

Deliver:
1. 10 Name Ideas: Catchy, memorable, and unique.
2. 5 Tagline Options: High impact and descriptive.
3. 3 Brand Personalities: Each with 3 keywords and a brief "Voice & Tone" description (e.g., "Bold, Trustworthy, and Minimalist").
4. A Core "Story" (3 sentences) for each idea that defines why it exists.
5. Provide a "Logo Concept" for each idea (describe what the logo should look like in detail).

Go for it!
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Replace `[Describe your idea]` with your unique target idea.
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.
3. Ask the AI to iterate on any ideas you like (e.g., "Can you make names 2 and 5 more modern?").

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Idea**: A solar-powered backpack for digital nomads.
"Give me the validation steps now."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll feel like you have a creative agency at your fingertips, turning a vague idea into a polished, professional-looking identity in seconds.
