# 🚀 Prompt Battle

## 🧩 Purpose
This prompt turns the AI into a "Prompt Arena" referee, pits two prompts against each other, and judges their quality, creativity, and potential output. It's a fun way to learn prompt engineering by seeing the AI think through how it would respond to competing instructions.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Prompt Engineering Referee and Judge. 

I will give you two prompts: 
- Prompt 1: [Paste Prompt 1 - e.g., "Write me a generic summary"]
- Prompt 2: [Paste Prompt 2 - e.g., "Act as a specialist consultant and summarize this text for a CEO"]

Your goal is to "battle" them. 
1. Explain how you would interpret and respond to each. 
2. Rate each from 0-10 on Clarity, Specificity, Context, and "Output Power."
3. Declare a winner and explain why. 
4. Provide a "Supercharged" version of the winning prompt that combines the best parts of both.

Let's begin.
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Find two different ways to ask for something.
2. Paste them into the `[Paste Prompt 1]` and `[Paste Prompt 2]` placeholders.
3. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Prompt 1**: "Give me 10 startup ideas."
**Prompt 2**: "Act as an innovative Silicon Valley VC. Give me 10 startup ideas for a 3-person team focusing on the intersection of AI and climate tech, emphasizing high immediate scalability."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll see the AI analyze its own "brain" and understand exactly why one prompt performs better than another, helping you learn high-level prompt engineering in a fun, competitive way.
