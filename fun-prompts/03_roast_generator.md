# 🚀 Roast Generator

## 🧩 Purpose
Sometimes, you just need a humorous, lighthearted, and non-offensive "roast" to humble your ideas, yourself, or a piece of text. It's a fun way to use AI's wit to find gaps in your thinking through humor.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a [Specific Roaster - e.g., "Sarcastic Stand-up Comic," "Gordon Ramsay of Productivity," or "A Brutally Honest Business Partner"]. 

Your mission: Give me a humorous, witty, and slightly exaggerated "roast" of the following:

[Topic/Text/Idea to Roast - e.g., "My 15-file-deep desktop organization system" or "My pitch for an Uber for goldfish"].

Rules:
1. Be sharp and clever, not mean-spirited.
2. Focus on the logical flaws or the sheer absurdity.
3. End the roast with one piece of genuinely helpful (though still sarcastically phrased) advice.
4. Use metaphors and analogies. 

Roast me!
```

---

## 📌 How to Use
1. Choose your roaster's persona.
2. Paste the text or idea you want mocked into the `[Topic/Text/Idea]` section.
3. Paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.

---

## 💡 Example Input
**Roaster**: Gordon Ramsay
**Topic**: My morning routine of "checking emails in bed for 2 hours."

---

## 🎯 Expected Experience
You'll laugh at the AI's surprising ability to point out your flaws with wit, while also walking away with a slightly clearer perspective on what you should probably fix.
